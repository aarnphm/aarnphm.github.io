> [!abstract]
>
> 4-tuple $(N, \Sigma, P, S)$
>
> - $N$: A finite set of non-terminal
> - $\Sigma$: A finite set of terminal $N \cap \Sigma = \emptyset$
> - $P$: is a finite subset of $N \times (N \cup \Sigma)^{*}$
> - $S$: $S \in N$, the start symbols
