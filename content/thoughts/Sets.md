Now a set is just a collection of different things. The thing is also called "elements" or members of the set.

Mostly of the set theory field largely studies possible axiom systems and its consequences. [ZFC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zermelo–Fraenkel_set_theory) is most commonly used axiom system for this.

It did began with Cantor, where we study infinite and finite set. (If you take any algebra classes, you might have heard of Cantor's set theory)

Now, [[thoughts/Wittgenstein#Russell's paradox and the vicious circle principle|Russell's paradox]] is also one problem you might encounter when first studying this part of mathematics. Set is useful tool that can be used to define [[thoughts/algebraic geometry|algebraic structures]] and mathematical spaces.
