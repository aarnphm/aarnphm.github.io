_the idea of any signals in time domain can be decomposed into sinusoidal components_

> [!math] definition
>
> The Fourier transform is given by the following:
>
> $$
> \displaystyle {\widehat {f}}(\xi )=\int _{-\infty }^{\infty }f(x)\ e^{-i2\pi \xi x}\,dx
> $$
