> A _proposition_ is a statement that can be evaluated to ==either true or false==, but **not both**

e.g:

| statement                   | proposition        |
| --------------------------- | ------------------ |
| "the sun rises in the east" | :white_check_mark: |
| 1+1 = 3                     | :white_check_mark: |
| x+1=2                       | :x:                |
| Go out and play             | :x:                |
