## communication protocol

- **primary channel**: Instagram
- **response window**: 24 hours (weekdays), 48 hours (weekends)
- **meeting platform**: teams or in-person. Prioritize asynchronous communication.
- **file sharing**: OneDrive

## meeting schedule

- **quorum**: minimum 4 members required for decisions
- **notes**: rotating responsibility, shared within 24 hours

## quality standards

1. all written work must be proofread before submission to team
2. citations in consistent format (APA/MLA as required)
3. no placeholder text or incomplete sections in shared drafts
4. all data and sources must be verifiable

## accountability measures

### performance expectations

- complete assigned tasks by internal deadlines
- communicate blockers immediately, not at deadline
- review teammates' work when assigned as backup

### escalation process

**level 1**: direct conversation between affected members
**level 2**: full team discussion at next meeting
**level 3**: formal warning documented in writing
**level 4**: professor notification and peer evaluation impact

### consequences for non-compliance

1. first violation: verbal warning, documented in meeting notes
2. second violation: written warning, copied to all members
3. third violation: reduced peer evaluation score recommendation
4. continued violations: professor consultation, potential removal from team credit

## conflict resolution

1. disputes first addressed between parties directly
2. if unresolved within 48 hours, escalate to full team vote
3. majority decision binding
4. if team cannot resolve, professor mediation requested

## amendment process

this contract may be amended by unanimous consent of all members, documented in writing.
