## break-even analysis

ex. Restaurant expansion

$$
\begin{aligned}
Rx &= F + Vx \\
\overline{x} &= \operatorname{BEP}(x) = \frac{F}{R-V} \\
\operatorname{BEP}(\$) &= \operatorname{BEP}(x) \times R
\end{aligned}
$$
