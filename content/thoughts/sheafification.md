It serves as the categorical _free_ construction, acting as the left adjoint to the inclusion functors from sheaves to presheaves. usually constructed in two-step process, also known as _plus construction_

## sheaves

Accordingly to [wikipedia](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sheaf_(mathematics)>), they are tools to track data systematically (think of abelian groups, sets, rings) that is attached to the open sets of a [[thoughts/topology|topological space]]
